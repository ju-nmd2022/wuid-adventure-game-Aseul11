The story befind the game

You were travelling with The Doctor and his companions on a planet called Alfava Metraxis, when you met a wheeping angels. These creatures can displace their victims backwards in time with a single touch, which exactly happened with The Doctor. You have no idea where and in what time he can be, but you should save him with a help of his other companions.

What should we do?

One of the companions knows the alien, Dorium Maldovar, who is in charge of rumours in the Universe, so he might know where The Doctor is. Let's visit him now!
